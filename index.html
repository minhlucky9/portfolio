About me

Like many young people, video games have been associated with my entire childhood. I have been exposed to these games since high school, from simple games on the black and white phone like Snake to the PC games with higher graphics like League of Legends or Genshin Impact.

For me, video games are not just entertaining games, it's also a whole new world where I can explore, discover, and create memorable memories with my close friends. Gradually, video games have become my passion, and I want to create many interesting and captivating games with my own hands. That's why I have chosen "Virtual Reality" as my field of study and research today and in the future.

At the moment, I have participated in several game and simulation projects developed using Unity3D, HTML and ThreeJS.

Main project
1. Digital exhibition "Vietnam's Hoang Sa and Truong Sa - Historical and legal evidence" (2020)
The Digital exhibition "Vietnam's Hoang Sa and Truong Sa - Historical and legal evidence" is a project in which I participated while working in the production team at CDIT. It is a project initiated by the Ministry of Information and Communications in 2020. Our team was responsible for creating a digital exhibition consisting of numerous pictures, models, and videos that provide evidence of Vietnam's sovereignty over seas and islands.
Programming language:
HTML, CSS, Javascript, ThreeJS
Link demo:
Web3D
2. Software system to support preclinical pediatric training based on virtual reality technology (2020)
This is a state-level project developed in collaboration with the National Children's Hospital, in which I participated while working in the production team at CDIT. The project involved simulating various emergency scenarios on patients and different medical techniques to save the patients, based on information provided by doctors. The primary objective of this simulation was to train students in making decisions during emergency cases. This simulation also supports multiplayer functionality.
Programming language:
Unity3D, C#, Photon Engine
Link demo:
private
3. The platform for spreading and promoting ethnic religion (2021)
This is a state-level project developed in collaboration with the journal VietnamNet, in which I participated while working in the production team at CDIT. The project aims to collect and present various digital data, such as images, 3D models, and 360-degree videos, showcasing the rich cultures of all ethnic minorities in Vietnam. The primary goal of this project is to promote and preserve the cultural heritage of these ethnic communities.
Programming language:
HTML, CSS, Javascript, ThreeJS
Link demo:
Web
4. Minigame Okeenga (2022)
The Minigame Okeenga is a project in which I participated while working in the production team at CDIT. It serves as a companion to the strategy game Ookeenga, produced by CROS Studio. The goal of this project is to create a village where players can utilize their characters from the main game to explore, undertake daily quests, and participate in events. The rewards obtained in the village will come in the form of items that can be used in the main game, and these rewards will be directly sent to their accounts.
Programming language:
HTML, CSS, Javascript, ThreeJS
Link demo:
Web3D
5. Cell Evolution - Global Game Jam Vietnam (2022)
Cell Evolution is a game that I developed with my friends during the Global Game Jam Vietnam 2023, completed in just one day. The theme of this game jam was "Root," which inspired us to create a game where cells fight against viruses while evolving little by little into a human, mirroring the evolutionary process of human beings.
Programming language:
Unity3D, C#

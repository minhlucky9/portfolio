INTRODUCTION
1 ABOUT FORBIDDEN LAND
Ookeenga: Forbidden Land is the place when NFT owners can fully view and test the 3D NFT Heroes they purchased from Ookeenga INO and earn cool rewards at the same time. It's also an opportunity for selected Whitelist players to experience the land feature of Ookeenga first-handed.
(*) The content of the Forbidden Land is not related to the official version of Ookeenga the Game.
2 EVENT TIME
First season of the Forbidden Land will be active from May 16 to May 23 2022. Once the event ends, the Land will be temporarily closed.
Lucky winners will then be announced on Ookeenga Global Telegram Channel.
3 HOW TO GET REWARDS? - FOR NFT OWNERS
STEP 01: Player collect ‘Blue Crystal’ item on the map and from NPC quest.
STEP 02: Exchange items
5 Blue Crystal = 1 Red Diamond
7 Red Diamond = 1 Lucky Ticket
Player may get maximum 2 Red Diamonds a day.
STEP 03: Wait for the Lottery Draw Event on Ookeenga Telegram Channel. Top Ranked Player and some random people with lucky numbers will receive big prizes.
4 HOW TO GET REWARDS? - FOR FREE USERS
STEP 01: Player collect ‘Blue Crystal’ item on the map and from NPC quest.
STEP 02: Exchange items
5 Blue Crystal = 1 Red Diamond
2 Red Diamond = 1 Lucky Ticket
Player may get maximum 2 Red Diamonds a day.
STEP 03: Wait for the Lottery Draw Event on Ookeenga Telegram Channel. Players with lucky numbers will receive decent prizes.
5 IMPORTANT NOTICE!
Blue Crystals are temporary. At 00:00 UTC everyday, your collected Blue Crystal will be reset.
Red Diamonds and Lucky Ticket are permanent. To prevent loss, please convert all Blue Crystals to Red Diamonds before the day ends.
Lucky Tickets awarded to NFT Owners and Free Users may look identical, but the item value is absolutely different! The prize pool for NFT Owners is multiple times bigger than Free User’s.
